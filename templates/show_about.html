About Me

Hi! I'm Hesam, pursuing a career change from Civil Engineering to Software Engineering. In the last four years (2019-2022), I worked on global projects, improved teamwork, and delivered projects on time and within budget. One significant challenge was a major high-speed railway project, where I succeeded by making precise assumptions, using empirical methods, and robust numerical models, delivering it within a year and by the deadline.

In engineering, I enjoyed achieving goals but wanted more challenging and demanding work. So, in February 2023, I started an intensive Software Engineering program at Springboard, reigniting my excitement for tackling challenges and growing. I'm eager to use my skills to develop practical solutions that address real-world issues, making a substantial contribution in this dynamic industry.

In my spare time, I love the feeling of being in nature and exploring new places, whether I'm hiking in the forests, biking through the countryside, or traveling to far-off destinations. Music is also a part of me that can never be taken away.

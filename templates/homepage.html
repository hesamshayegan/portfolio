MoviesBox

MoviesBox is a website designed to assist users in discovering new movies according to their personal preferences. It employs a robust machine learning algorithm that applies content-based filtering methods. The algorithm has been trained on a comprehensive collection of 10,000 diverse movies. By analyzing the movies that users enjoy the most, MoviesBox can provide accurate and appealing movie recommendations.

Tech Stack: Python, Flask, PostgreSQL, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap

JeoParty

JeoParty is a trivia game that will test your knowledge and challenge your brain. In this two-player mode, you and your friend will compete to answer questions and earn points. The team with the highest score at the end of the game wins! The app randomly selects 6 categories and 5 clues per category, so you'll never know what you're going to get. Will you be able to answer questions about history, science, pop culture, or something else entirely? Only one way to find out! JeoParty is the perfect game for a party or a night with friends. Gather your crew and get ready to have some fun!

Tech Stack: JavaScript, jQuery, HTML, CSS

StarWars Challenge

The Star Wars Challenge is an interactive web-based game where players test their memory by flipping cards and matching pairs of Star Wars main characters. Players are presented with a grid of cards, each depicting a different character from the Star Wars universe. The objective is to find and match all the pairs of characters by flipping two cards at a time. The game requires concentration, observation, and a good memory to remember the positions of the cards. It's a fun and engaging way to challenge your knowledge of the iconic Star Wars characters while enjoying a classic memory card game.

Tech Stack: Vanilla JavaScript, HTML, CSS
